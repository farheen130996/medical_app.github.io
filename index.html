Heart attack Symptoms


1. Chest pain

2. Pressure or tightness or a squeezing or aching sensation in the center of the chest.

3. Pain or discomfort that spreads to the shoulder, arm, back, neck, jaw, teeth or occasionally upper abdomen.

4. Nausea, indigestion, heartburn or abdominal pain. Shortness of breath.

First Aid for Heart attack


1. Consume Aspirin

2. Call Ambulance

3. Take any prescribed chest pain medication

4. Rest in a comfortable position and wait for the ambulance to arrive also Loosen tight clothing
